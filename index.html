About Me

Hi, I’m Thomas Brown, also known online as Lifeport-z.

I’m an aspiring 3D Environment, Prop, and Poster Artist with about 3 years of experience using Blender for modeling and animating.

While I’m still an amateur and continuously learning, I’m deeply passionate about 3D art and dedicated to improving my skills every day. Creating detailed and immersive 3D products that bring digital worlds and stories to life is something I truly enjoy.

My work ranges from building complex environments and designing unique props to producing striking posters. I believe that combining creativity with technical skills is key to making impactful digital art, and I’m committed to developing both sides.

I understand that I’m at the beginning of my journey, and I welcome constructive feedback and collaboration to help me grow as an artist. Every project is an opportunity to learn something new, and I’m excited about what the future holds.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and connect with me if you’re interested in working together or sharing insights. Follow me on Lifeport-z to see my latest work and progress.

My Goals

My ultimate goal is to work on games that create powerful emotional experiences, like the Outlast and Souls series. I want to be part of teams that craft worlds players remember for a lifetime.

I am constantly pushing myself to improve so that no matter how challenging the task, my work is always of the highest quality. I aim for a standard where every asset I create feels intentional, polished, and impactful.

Beyond technical mastery, I hope to find and build friendships within the game development community — people who are kind, caring, and passionate about creating meaningful work together.
